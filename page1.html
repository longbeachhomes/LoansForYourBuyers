Cesar Marin

Loan Officer, Tax Consultant

When homeowners experience economic needs or severe life stressors due to their home payments, it is not uncommon for their lives to become difficult. My great passion is bringing solutions to people who are going through a stressful experience due to that. I help my clients, who include home buyers, homeowners, and parents, to reach a better living so they can strengthen their relationships with their families and so they can get for themselves the peace and minduful state of mind that can only be acquired when their economic difficulties are taken care of.

I know that no single solution is the right one for every individual, and so I have been trained in a range of programs including Conventional, FHA, VA and USDH that are designed for the different types of home financing.

My educational background includes financial and tax preparation education. I have a B.A. in Sociology from the National University of Mexico, Mexico and finance training from Penn Valley College.
